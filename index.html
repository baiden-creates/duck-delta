Sw
Auto
Sweep
Price interacts with a marked zone while a momentum condition is satisfied. Detected automatically on candle close. Works on both bullish and bearish configurations. Fires once per cycle.
Tr
Manual
Trendline
Draw structure directly on the chart to mark the prevailing trend of the move. Arm the detector with one click. Only becomes available after Sw fires - keeps the pipeline in sequence.
Br
Auto
Structure Break
A candle closes on the other side of the drawn structure on the selected timeframe. Candle-close confirmed - no wick noise. Requires both prior steps to be complete before it can arm.
En
Auto
Entry
A momentum indicator crosses a key threshold and confirms on the following close. Two-candle confirmation eliminates noise. If the condition was already satisfied at Br, it waits for a fresh signal.

Real-time liquidation stream from Binance. Fires an alert and sound when a significant event hits inside a drawn zone. Visual flow display updates in real time as data arrives.
